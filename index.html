VTU Syllabus APP

VTU Syllabus App is the first iOS app to view syllabus details of Visvesvaraya Technological University, Belgaum.
This app doesn't require internet connection and all the syllabus related data is stored offline. Currently the app supports syllabus of:
  • B.E
    1. 1st year(all branches).
    2. ECE
    3. TCE
    4. CSE
    5. ISE
    6. EEE
    7. Mechanical
    8. Civil
    9. Bio Tech
  • MBA (All Branches)
